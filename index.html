Top 3 places you must visit in Bago

Kanbawza Thadi Palace

A stunning golden reconstruction of King Bayinnaung’s 16th-century royal residence. It showcases the Great Audience Hall, replica Lion Throne, and a museum housing original, 400-year-old teak wood pillars.

Kyaik Pun Pagoda

Famous for four 27-meter-tall seated Buddha statues facing the cardinal directions. Built by Mon sisters, the site is tied to a legend where one statue crumbled when a sister married.

Shwemawdaw Pagoda

Myanmar’s tallest pagoda at 114 meters. This 1,000-year-old "Great Golden God" houses sacred hair relics and features a massive brick spire toppled by a historic 1917 earthquake at its base.
